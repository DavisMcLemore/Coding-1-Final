About Me

Music has been a passion of mine for my whole life. About six months ago I decided that I would learn how to play guitar. On top of that I've learned how to play piano and ukulele. Learning how to play instruments must be one of the best things that has happened to me in my lifetime. It has helped me cope with the chaos and unpredictability of this world. My respect for good music has been elevated because I have a better understanding on how it is made. I plan to continue pursuing my passion for music no matter what the future holds.
